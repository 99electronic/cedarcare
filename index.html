Training the next generation of doctors.

At CedarCare, we are committed to not only training the next generation of doctors but also bridging the gap between the young and the elderly. Many of our health coaches are highly motivated premedical students who want to someday go to medical school. From our experience, they provide exceptional medical care to our patients by building companionship and often genuine friendships with our elderly patients. We are honored to have our physicians mentor our health coaches and watch many of them get into medical school to become doctors themselves.

Services

We are proud to offer the following services to our valued patients.

Remote Patient Monitoring

Our physicians work around the clock to help you manage your blood pressure, weight, and blood sugars at home

Prescriptions and Refills

We work closely with pharmacies in the community to provide the lowest cost medications and generic drugs to save you money

Chronic Care Management

Our team of physicians and health coaches are passionate about encouraging exercise, dietary changes, and medication adjustments for our patients with chronic conditions

Precision Medicine

When possible and with patient consent, we can use genetic testing to help prevent, diagnose, and treat diseases in a more personalized and precise manner

Access to Care

At CedarCare, we are committed to helping those with access to care issues such as the elderly and those with financial hardships

Referrals to Specialists

We work closely with cardiologists, dermatologists, ENTs, oncologists, and many other specialists at Valley Medical Center and local hospitals and clinics
